A Legacy of Learning & Character

Since 1992, Seedling School Jaipur has been a beacon of academic excellence and holistic child development in Rajasthan. Part of the renowned Seedling Group of Schools, we are managed by Mahima Shiksha Samiti and have grown into one of Jaipur's most trusted educational institutions.

Our co-educational day school welcomes students from Nursery (Play Group) right through to Class XII, offering a seamless educational journey in a nurturing, values-driven environment. With campuses in Jawahar Nagar and Durgapura, we serve thousands of families across Jaipur.

We believe every child is a unique seedling — with the right environment, mentorship, and resources, each one grows into a confident, compassionate, and globally aware leader.
